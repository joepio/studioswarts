Napapijri Bread&Butter Berlin 2017

The remake of Antonio Vivaldi’s world-famous ‘The Four Seasons’ by four music artists formed the backbone for this layered 360 degree video, sound and sensory set-up at Bread&Butter Berlin. By designing a walk-in experience where all seasons and Napapijri’s juxtaposition of urban & nature was brought to life and showcased the live premiere of all tracks.
